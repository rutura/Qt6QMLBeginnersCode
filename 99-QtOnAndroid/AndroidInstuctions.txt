	. Installing tools for android dev in Qt Creator
	
		. Prepare two folders where you'll install the SDK and the JDK
			. The JDK gives you the Java SDK and the Jre to run java apps
			. The Android SDK gives android utilities on top of the JDK
			. I created an Android folder in C, and in there I put the folders : 
					. JDK
					. SDK
		. In Qt Creator go to Tools->Options->Devices->Android
